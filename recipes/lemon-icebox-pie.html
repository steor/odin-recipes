Lemon Icebox Pie Recipe

A family favourite when a no-bake, fast pie is needed.

Ingredients

  • 1 x (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust
  • 2 x (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 x (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 x lemons, juiced
  • 1 x teaspoon lemon zest

Steps

  1. In a medium mixing bowl, beat cream cheese until fluffy.
  2. Add condensed milk, lemon juice, and lemon rind.
  3. Mix until smooth. Pour mixture into crust.
  4. Refrigerate at least 2 hours before serving.
  5. Garnish with whipped cream and mint leaves if desired.
